Safety Raceway

Race as an original yellow-and-purple mustached driver in a small kart. The course is playful and bright, but the lesson is serious: fatigue and microsleeps can quickly take away control.

  • Automatic forward motion; steer with WASD, arrow keys, or touch buttons.
  • Avoid banana peels and green shell roadway hazards; strike icons show impacts and near misses.
  • The full run lasts exactly 120 seconds, and microsleeps begin 5 seconds after the race starts.
  • Use P or Esc to pause if you need a break.
  • No copyrighted characters, names, music, logos, tracks, or assets are used.

Training Debrief

  • Fatigue reduces reaction time.
  • Microsleeps can happen without warning.
  • Even a one-second lapse can cause lane departure or collision.
  • Coffee, loud music, and open windows are not substitutes for sleep.
  • If you are too tired to drive safely, stop, rest, and notify your supervisor.
